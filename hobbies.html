Knitting

I love to knit! My Freshman year of college I really started getting into it and have since learned many different ways to knit. I have created so many beautiful scarves, mittens, hats, and blankets that I have run out of people to gift them to. My best friend from my hometown and I get together a couple of times a week and knit while catching up. At the end of each year, we donate everything we've made local children's hospitals and other charities in the area.

Traveling

Ever since I was little I have always had a desire to travel. My parents are two people who have always spent their extra money on vacations. In 18 years of life I have visited Disney World more times than I can count, every state in New England, Mexico, New York City, New Jersey, and many other locations outside of the United States.
